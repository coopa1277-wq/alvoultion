Questions, Answered

What everyone asks before switching

Will HAVEN replace my team or my receptionist?
No. HAVEN takes the repetitive work off their plate — answering the same questions, sending reminders, chasing invoices, writing reports — so your people spend their day on the work that actually needs a human: relationships, judgment and the jobs that need experience. Your scheduler still schedules. Your bookkeeper still keeps the books. Their job gets better, not gone.
Does Alvolution bring in new leads, or just manage the ones I have?
Both. Alongside HAVEN, Alvolution runs done-for-you marketing campaigns that generate new, qualified enquiries for your business — then HAVEN answers, qualifies and books every one of them automatically. You get fresh demand coming in the front door and a system that makes sure none of it slips through the cracks.
Will my customers feel like they're talking to a robot?
Most customers care about one thing: how fast someone picks up and how helpful they are. HAVEN answers in under two seconds, is always polite, never leaves anyone on hold, and books the appointment on the spot. When a call needs a real conversation, it hands the customer straight to a person. The result is a better experience, not a colder one.
How is this different from ServiceTitan or JobNimbus?
The big platforms give you a generic toolbox and leave you to spend months programming it around your business. HAVEN is built around the exact processes you already run and automates them the same way you would by hand — so you get value in days, not after a long technical onboarding.
Isn't this just another bill on top of everything I pay for?
It's the opposite. If you're paying for a receptionist, a scheduling tool, an invoicing app and stock-tracking software, HAVEN does all four in one place for one flat fee — and frees up the people you already pay to do higher-value work. Most businesses spend less overall after switching, not more. It clears costs out instead of piling them on.
Can HAVEN do the actual trade work or give professional advice?
No — and it isn't meant to. HAVEN is not a licensed electrician, a certified roofer or a qualified plumber. It answers the phone, books the job and handles the admin. Your expert shows up and does the work. The years of trade experience your team has built is something HAVEN will never replace.
What does HAVEN actually handle day to day?
Inbound calls via an AI voice agent, sub-60-second lead follow-up, appointment scheduling and reminders, invoicing on job close, inventory tracking, report generation, CRM follow-up and automated marketing campaigns — all through a single platform you operate by simply telling it what you need.
